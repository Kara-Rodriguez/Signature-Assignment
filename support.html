Available Services

    • Low-Cost Spay/Neuter: Appointment-only (Tue/Fri)
    • Food Pantry: 1st & 3rd Wed, 5:00 – 6:30 PM
    • Behavior Helpline: Mon–Thu, 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM

    Helpful links: City Animal Services Warm Hearts Rescue
